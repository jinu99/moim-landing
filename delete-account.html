Account Deletion Information

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your moim account directly from the app. Follow these simple steps:

1
Open Settings

Launch the moim app and tap on the Settings tab.

2
Go to Account Management

Select "Account Management" from the settings menu.

3
Request Deletion

Tap "Delete Account" and follow the confirmation prompts.

4
Confirm

Your account will be permanently deleted immediately.

What Happens When You Delete

When you delete your account:

Permanently Deleted

  • Your profile information (name, photo, interests, bio)
  • Your login credentials and authentication data
  • Your membership in all groups

This action cannot be undone. Please make sure you want to proceed.
