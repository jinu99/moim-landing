Privacy Policy

Effective from November 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Heyjaws ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our Moim application and related services (the "Service").

1 Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information such as nickname, email address, password, profile photo, interests, and bio
  • Content you create including posts, photos, videos, comments, and messages
  • Customer support inquiries including your email and message content

Information Collected Automatically

  • Location data via GPS or IP address (with your permission)
  • Device information including model, OS, and advertising identifiers (ADID/IDFA)
  • Usage data such as access logs, session duration, and app usage frequency

2 How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To provide and maintain our Service under our contract with you
  • Consent-Based Processing: For purposes you have explicitly agreed to
  • Legitimate Interests: Fraud prevention, service improvement, and analytics
  • Legal Compliance: To fulfill legal obligations

3 Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except:

  • With other users as part of the Service's community features
  • With service providers for cloud hosting, analytics, and other operational needs
  • When required by law or legal process
  • In connection with a business transfer, merger, or acquisition

4 International Data Transfers

Your information may be stored and processed in various countries where our facilities or service providers are located. We implement appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

5 Additional Rights for Malaysia Users

Under the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA), Malaysian users have specific rights including explicit consent requirements and the right to restrict processing.

While providing personal information is voluntary, certain features may be limited without it.

6 Data Retention

We retain your personal information while your account is active. Upon request, we will delete your data unless we are legally required to retain it for a longer period.

7 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke previously given consent

Exercise these rights through Account Settings or by contacting us.

8 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption and access controls. However, no system is completely secure.

9 Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 16 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10 Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the Service or via email.
